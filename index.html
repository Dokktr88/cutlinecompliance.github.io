About Cutline Compliance

Cutline develops educational tools and frameworks that help non- and pre-regulated operations build foundational food-safety systems. We emphasize evidence-based instruction on hazard analysis, documentation, and risk literacy—using publicly available information.

Scope: We focus exclusively on education and program literacy. We do not perform regulatory advisory work or representation.

FAQs

Do you work with USDA-regulated plants?

We provide education and literacy support only (no regulatory advisory). Teams use our resources to understand concepts and build foundational skills.

Do you create HACCP plans?

We educate teams on hazard analysis and documentation basics using public information. Any formal plan development, validation, or regulatory decisions remain with your organization.
