About Cutline Compliance

Cutline develops educational tools and frameworks that help non- and pre-regulated operations build foundational food-safety systems. We emphasize evidence-based instruction on hazard analysis, documentation, and risk literacy—using publicly available information. We also provide consultation services for non-USDA-regulated operations such as restaurants, retail/commissary kitchens, caterers, and food trucks.

Scope for USDA-regulated plants: Education and program literacy only. We do not perform regulatory advisory work or representation.

FAQs

Do you consult with restaurants and food-service operations?

Yes. We consult with non-USDA operations (restaurants, retail / commissaries, caterers, food trucks) providing SOPs, logs, and team literacy.

Do you work with USDA-regulated plants?

We provide education and literacy support only. No regulatory advisory or representation.

Do you create HACCP plans?

We educate on hazard analysis using public sources. Formal validation or regulatory decisions remain with your organization.

How fast can we start?

Discovery can begin this week. Then we build a learning plan and immediate quick-win steps.
