WHY CUTLINE IS DIFFERENT

Most consultants focus on paperwork. Cutline focuses on whether the system behind it works.

Documents matter. But documents are evidence — not armor. Cutline helps food businesses think through the people, processes, decisions, verification habits, and documentation logic that determine whether a food safety system can actually hold up.

Systems over shortcuts

Templates can support a system, but they cannot replace understanding. Cutline starts with how the system actually functions.

Readiness over reaction

Inspection, audit, and customer pressure should not be the first time weak points become visible. Readiness is built before the event.

Function over appearance

Clean records do not prove control. Strong systems create repeatable performance under real operating conditions.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Clear boundaries. Practical use.

Is CRD a compliance score?

No. CRD is an educational system-pattern visibility tool. It does not score compliance, issue approvals, or make regulatory determinations.

What is the best starting point?

For most customers, Compliance Readiness Fundamentals is the best starting point. It teaches how food safety systems are evaluated and why problems often appear before inspection ever happens.

What does the CRD report provide?

The report provides structured visibility into system patterns, pressure signals, readiness indicators, and documentation-reality gaps for internal discussion.

Can I use CRD more than once?

Yes. One-time access provides one official report. The professional subscription provides recurring access and up to 4 official reports per billing cycle.

Does Cutline replace legal, regulatory, audit, or inspection advice?

No. Cutline products are educational and readiness-focused. They do not replace legal counsel, regulatory advice, audits, inspections, certification, or qualified professional review.

What weak point is your system normalizing?

Most food safety failures do not begin at inspection. They begin as small pressure signals — unclear authority, weak feedback, documentation gaps, repeated workarounds, or problems that slowly become normal.

The Compliance Readiness Diagnostic helps surface those patterns before an audit, inspection, customer requirement, or operational failure exposes them for you.
