Why Cutline is Different

Not paperwork. Control.

Most “consulting” produces binders. Cutline builds detection and response systems your team can run every day.

Plain language. Real workflow.

SOPs should be work instructions. Logs should fit the line. If it can’t be executed, it’s not a system.

Public-source, defensible education.

We teach concepts using publicly available standards and patterns—so your team learns the “why” and applies it consistently.

If an inspector or auditor finds something before your system does, that’s not bad luck. It’s a lagging indicator. Healthy systems catch small signals early, internally, and repeatedly.

About Cutline Compliance

Cutline develops educational tools and frameworks that help non- and pre-regulated operations build foundational food-safety systems. We emphasize public-source instruction on hazard analysis, documentation, and risk literacy. We also provide consultation services for non-USDA-regulated operations such as restaurants, retail/commissary kitchens, caterers, and food trucks.

Do you consult with restaurants and food-service operations?
