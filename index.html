FAQs

Do you work with USDA-regulated plants?

We provide educational guidance and readiness support. Work is designed to avoid conflicts with federal ethics rules—no representation or advocacy before the agency.

Do you build HACCP plans?

We help you scaffold documentation and align teams with scientific justification and practical controls. Final validation and regulatory determinations are your responsibility.

How fast can we start?

Discovery can happen this week. From there, we sequence a rapid review and begin the highest-leverage fixes first.
