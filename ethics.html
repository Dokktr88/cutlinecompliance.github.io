PRODUCT BOUNDARIES

What Cutline products are — and what they are not.

The following boundaries apply across Cutline Compliance training, diagnostics, educational materials, and customer-facing product workflows.

Education & Readiness

Cutline products are designed to help users understand food safety systems, readiness patterns, documentation logic, pressure points, and operational signals.

System Visibility

CRD and related tools surface system-pattern signals for internal discussion. They do not produce a compliance score, regulatory conclusion, certification, or approval.

Professional Boundaries

Cutline products do not replace legal advice, regulatory advice, audits, inspections, certification, qualified professional review, or internal management responsibility.

APPROPRIATE USE

Use Cutline tools to support internal readiness discussion.

Cutline products are most useful when customers use them to understand patterns, ask better questions, strengthen internal awareness, and support responsible planning.

Use reports as discussion tools CRD output should support internal readiness review, not act as a final determination.
Answer from operational reality Diagnostics and training work best when users focus on what actually happens under pressure.
Protect internal information Customers are responsible for storing, sharing, and managing exported reports appropriately.
Keep responsibility internal Education and diagnostic tools support understanding, but they do not transfer ownership of the system.

Do Not Use Cutline Products As

Not a Score No Compliance Rating

CRD does not score compliance or produce regulatory conclusions.

Not an Audit No Certification

Cutline products do not replace audits, inspections, certification, or professional review.

Not Agency Authority No Official Interpretation

Cutline does not issue regulatory decisions, approvals, or official agency interpretations.

SUPPORT BOUNDARY

What Cutline support can help with.

Product support is focused on access, billing flow, account navigation, app workflow, documentation, and general product questions.

Covered Support

Account access, billing flow, report credits, dashboard navigation, download issues, product questions, and app workflow problems.

Outside Product Support

Facility-specific compliance, legal, audit, inspection, corrective action, certification, or regulatory questions must be handled outside the CRD app and outside product support.
