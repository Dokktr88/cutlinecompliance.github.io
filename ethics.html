ETHICS & SCOPE

Clear boundaries protect the customer, the product, and the integrity of the work.

Cutline provides independent education and system-pattern visibility tools. It does not act as a regulator, attorney, auditor, certification body, or representative before a government agency.

ELIGIBILITY BOUNDARY

USDA-FSIS regulated establishments

Cutline Compliance does not provide facility-specific consulting, regulatory strategy, corrective-action guidance, representation, or regulatory interpretation to establishments currently regulated by USDA-FSIS.

General educational products may only be used within the stated scope and eligibility conditions for that product. Questions about eligibility should be submitted before purchase.

Do not submit

  • Confidential establishment records
  • Noncompliance records or enforcement correspondence for review
  • Facility-specific corrective-action requests
  • Requests for regulatory interpretation or strategy
  • Requests for representation before USDA-FSIS

WHAT CUTLINE PROVIDES

Education and structured system visibility.

Public-source education

Independent educational content based on publicly available regulations, guidance, and systems concepts.

Diagnostic pattern visibility

Structured tools that help eligible users discuss patterns, pressure signals, and documentation-reality gaps.

Product support

Account, access, billing, technical, accessibility, and general product-use support within stated product boundaries.

DATA & PRIVACY

Use business-appropriate information only.

Customers should avoid entering unnecessary personal, confidential, proprietary, or sensitive information. Do not upload or disclose internal records unless a product specifically supports and requires that action.

  • Do not enter passwords or payment-card details into support messages.
  • Do not submit sensitive employee or consumer information.
  • Store exported reports according to your internal document-control practices.
  • Use the secure billing processor and account portal for payment and subscription management.

Customer responsibility

Customers remain responsible for their operation, regulatory obligations, food-safety decisions, professional advice, and use of any product output.

INDEPENDENCE

Independent work—not agency communication.

Cutline Compliance is an independent business. Its products, frameworks, educational content, and opinions do not represent USDA, FSIS, FDA, or any other government agency. References to public regulations or guidance do not create an official interpretation.
